Frequently Asked Questions

NutriTrack uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is the number of calories your body needs at rest. We then apply an activity multiplier based on your activity level to determine your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Finally, we adjust this number based on your goal (weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain) to arrive at your daily calorie target.
